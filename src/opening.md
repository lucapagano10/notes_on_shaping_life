# Opening


I've been writing things down for a while. In journals, in notes on my phone, in conversations with friends, in the margins of books that hit me at the right time. Most of it was messy. Contradictory. Half-formed. But some of it, over time, started somewhat rhymed.

This is what came out.

If something here hits you, good. If something doesn't, skip it. The only thing I'd ask is that you read it slowly. The stuff that matters tends to land on the second read, not the first.

So my only ask: read it slowly, word by word.

Life is not a puzzle you solve. It's something you shape. These are my notes on shaping it.
