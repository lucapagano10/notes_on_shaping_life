# On Attention


What you pay attention to, expands. Never thought of how crazy it is when you really want something, let’s say, a car, and all you can see are cars everywhere? That’s your subconscious giving power to what you pay attention to. The thing is, most of the time, your attention flows without you being aware. It just gets grabbed by whatever's loudest. Whatever's in front of you.

Every action is a vote for the type of person you want to become. You're casting votes all day long. Most people don't realize they're voting. We know this. But there's something about it that's hard to feel. Because the shift is so slow. You don't notice the erosion until years later, when you look up and think: wait, how did I end up here?

Your attention gets more limited every year. And everything around you is engineered to steal it. Once you're distracted enough, you just assume that's what life feels like.

What you focus on expands. Focus on problems, more problems appear. Focus on what's possible, more possibility shows up. The mind finds what it's looking for. Pay attention to what you pay attention to. That's where everything starts.
