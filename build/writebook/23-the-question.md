# The Question

This is your life. What do you want to pay attention to?

final- notes on shaping life                                                                              17

That question is the thread that runs through everything here. The eagle that never looked up. The joy you're suspicious of. The doors that close while you stand at the crossroads. It all comes back to attention. Where you point it decides what grows. What grows decides who you become. I don't have the answer. I'm not sure I ever will. But I keep asking the question, and every time I ask it, something shifts. Not dramatically. Not all at once. Just enough to notice. Just enough to correct course before ten years go by and I'm somewhere I didn't choose. The question is simple, the answer is about the living.
