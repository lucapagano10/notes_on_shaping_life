# On Creating

Creativity is a strange coin. The more you spend, the more you have. Here's something that haunts me a little. Most people die with their best work still inside them. Ideas they never tried. Words they never said. Things they kept meaning to make, but the timing was never quite right. The conditions were never right. Here's the truth. The conditions are never right. The goal is not to arrive at death with your potential carefully preserved. Risks avoided. Nothing left on the table. The goal is to spend it all.

Create. Ship. Say the thing. Make the attempt. It's better to make something bad than nothing at all. You can improve something bad. You can't improve something that doesn't exist. Get every idea out of your head and into the world. Not because every idea is good. But because the good ones and the bad ones are all tangled up together. The only way to find the good ones is to get them all out. Focus on making things rather than consuming them. The ratio matters. Most people spend ninety percent of their time taking in other people's work and ten percent making their own. Flip it, even a little, and everything changes. If you have a positive thought about someone, don't keep it to yourself. Share it immediately. Encouragement defies the laws of physics: when you give energy, you also receive it. Creating isn't just about objects and projects. It's about putting things into the world that weren't there before. A word of encouragement counts. A note counts. A conversation you initiate instead of waiting for counts. There's something I found about happiness that connects to this. The key is doing things for your future self, and when you get to the future, being grateful for what your past self did. Writing is like that. Happiness is taking the time to write something today, and tomorrow taking the time to read it. So. What are you holding back? What are you saving for later? There is no later. There's only now. And the story you tell yourself about later. Whatever is in you, make it real. Die empty.
